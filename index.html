Grab is a company that offers a service that solves many transportation challenges through the use of one mobile app, and serves B2C and B2B customers. It promises the customer for safety, fast, and secure delivery with low price. The services include taxi service, food delivery, and parcel delivery (in case you forgot your items at home or anywhere). What is unique about grab is that it works with the existing taxi ecosystem in the countries it is operating in. This makes the price more reasonable. For grab taxi, you can also pick who you want to drive with.
